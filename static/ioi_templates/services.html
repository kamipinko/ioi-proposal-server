Our Services

We offer a range of support services built around each individual's needs. Contact us to learn which services may be right for you.

What We Provide

Support Tailored to You

Every service we offer is designed to help individuals live more independently and with greater quality of life. All services are delivered with dignity and respect.

Personal Supports

Eligibility: Adults enrolled in Maryland DDA Medicaid Waiver  |  Setting: In-home

Personal Supports provides one-on-one assistance with the activities of daily living — including personal hygiene, meal preparation, medication reminders, household tasks, and community access. A trained support specialist works alongside the individual in their home on a regular schedule, focusing on building independence and confidence over time. Services are flexible and can be adjusted as the individual's needs and goals change. This service is available to adults enrolled in the Maryland DDA Community Pathways Waiver or Family Supports Waiver.

Community Development Services (CDS)

Eligibility: Adults with DDA enrollment  |  Setting: Community-based

Community Development Services is a structured day program designed to help individuals build practical life skills, form social connections, and participate meaningfully in their community. Participants engage in learning activities, volunteer opportunities, recreational outings, and skill-building workshops in real-world community settings. Staff work with each person individually to support their specific goals — whether that's learning to use public transit, managing money, or building friendships. CDS is not a day center — it happens out in the community, where it matters most.

Supported Employment

Eligibility: Adults 18+ with a disability impacting employment  |  Setting: Community / Workplace

Supported Employment helps individuals with disabilities find, prepare for, and maintain competitive, integrated jobs in the community. Our job coaches work directly with each person — helping them identify their interests and strengths, apply for positions, complete job training, and navigate workplace expectations. We also work with employers to build understanding and ensure a successful match. Our goal is real employment at real wages, not sheltered work or unpaid internships. Services continue as long as the individual needs ongoing support to stay employed.

Respite Care

Eligibility: Families and caregivers of individuals with DDA enrollment  |  Setting: In-home / Community

Respite Care provides temporary, planned relief for family members and unpaid caregivers who provide daily support to an individual with a disability. A trained Inspired Options staff member steps in so that caregivers can rest, attend appointments, work, or simply recharge. Respite can be scheduled in advance or arranged on short notice depending on availability. Taking care of yourself makes you a better caregiver — and we are here to make that possible. Services may be covered under the Maryland DDA Family Supports Waiver.

Behavioral Support Services

Eligibility: Current IOI clients with identified behavioral support needs  |  Setting: In-home / Community

Behavioral Support Services provides evidence-based, individualized support for individuals who exhibit challenging behaviors that affect their safety, relationships, or daily functioning. Our behavioral support specialists conduct assessments, develop positive behavioral support plans, and train caregivers and staff to implement strategies consistently across settings. We focus on understanding the reasons behind behavior — not just managing it — so that lasting change is possible. All behavioral support plans are developed in collaboration with the individual and their support team and reviewed regularly for effectiveness.

Getting Started

How to Access Services

To find out if you or your loved one is eligible for our services, the first step is to contact us. Our team will ask a few simple questions and walk you through the next steps.

We work with individuals, families, caregivers, and referring providers. There is no cost to inquire.

What to Expect

  1. Contact us by phone, email, or form
  2. We will schedule a free conversation
  3. We will review your needs and goals together
  4. We will develop a care plan that fits you
  5. Services begin on an agreed schedule

Questions About Our Services?

We are happy to explain what each service involves and help you figure out what is the best fit.
