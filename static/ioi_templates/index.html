Our Mission

To provide high-quality, person-centered care that empowers individuals with mental and cognitive disabilities to live with dignity, independence, and belonging in their communities.

Why Inspired Options

We Put People First

Everything we do starts with the person we serve — their goals, their needs, and their potential.

🤝

Person-Centered Care

We build care plans around each individual — not a one-size-fits-all model. Your goals guide our work.

🏠

Community-Based Support

We help individuals stay connected to their communities, families, and daily lives in meaningful ways.

🔒

Safe & Trusted

Our team is trained, background-checked, and committed to the safety and well-being of everyone we serve.

What We Offer

Our Services

We offer a range of support services designed to meet people where they are and help them grow.

🧠

Personal Supports

One-on-one in-home assistance with daily living tasks — including personal care, medication reminders, meal preparation, and household routines. Tailored to each person's goals.

Learn More
💬

Community Development Services

A structured day program helping individuals build life skills, develop social connections, and participate meaningfully in their community through activities and group experiences.

Learn More
🌱

Supported Employment

Job coaching, vocational training, and ongoing workplace support to help individuals with disabilities find and maintain competitive, integrated employment in the community.

Learn More
